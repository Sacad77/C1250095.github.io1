How to Use This Website

Welcome to the Language Learning Center help guide. This page explains how to navigate and use each section of our website.

Navigation

Use the navigation bar at the top of every page to access:

  • Home: Overview of our courses, teachers, and testimonials.
  • Gallery: View images of our classes, events, and facilities.
  • Booking: Enroll in a course using our online form.
  • About: Learn our history, mission, and meet the team.
  • Help: This page – find answers to common questions.

Gallery Page

Browse through our image gallery. Each image has a caption. Below the gallery, you'll find the Course Schedule Table which shows all available courses, teachers, days, times, and fees. The table has:

  • Sticky header for easy reference.
  • Alternating row colors for better readability.
  • Hover effects on rows.
  • A footer showing total monthly fees.

Booking Form

Fill in the booking form to enroll in a course. The form includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone, age.
  • Course Details: Language and level selection.
  • Gender & Hobbies: Radio buttons and checkboxes.
  • Address & Comments: Text areas for additional information.
  • Schedule Preference: Preferred time and start date.
  • Agreement: Checkbox to accept terms.

All required fields are marked with an asterisk (*) and include HTML5 validation (required, pattern, minlength, maxlength).

About Page

Learn about our history, mission, vision, objectives, and services. You'll also find a profile card for the student who built this project, Sa'ad Said Araye.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I enroll? Go to the Booking page and fill in the form.
  • Are classes online or in-person? We offer both options.
  • What languages do you teach? English, Arabic, French, Turkish, Chinese, and Spanish.
  • Do you offer certificates? Yes, upon successful completion.
  • Can I change my schedule? Contact our support team.
